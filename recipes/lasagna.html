Delicious Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe is sure to be a hit at your next family dinner or gathering. It's packed with delicious layers of noodles, sauce, and cheese, and it's easy to make ahead of time. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) pasta sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 package (8 ounces) lasagna noodles
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the pasta sauce, diced tomatoes, dried basil, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine, and let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese and 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, followed by a layer of the cheese mixture. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used up, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
